About Waypoints

Our Mission

At Waypoints, we're on a mission to transform how regions leverage their unique outdoor assets to drive sustainable economic growth, foster community engagement, and attract innovation. We believe that the path to regional prosperity lies in connecting the dots between technology, outdoor recreation, and local businesses.

Our approach is built on three core pillars:

Discover

We help regions identify and activate their underutilized outdoor assets, transforming them into engines for economic development.

Connect

We build bridges between outdoor enthusiasts, local businesses, and community initiatives, creating an ecosystem of mutual growth.

Thrive

We implement sustainable solutions that deliver measurable economic impact while preserving the natural and cultural resources that make each region unique.

Our Story

Waypoints was founded in 2024 by a team of outdoor enthusiasts, technologists, and economic development experts who saw an opportunity to help regions unlock their full potential.

After years of working with communities across the country, we noticed a common thread: regions with exceptional outdoor assets often struggled to translate those resources into economic opportunities. At the same time, we witnessed how technology could bridge gaps and create new possibilities.

Waypoints was born from the belief that by bringing together the right mix of strategic planning, technology solutions, and community engagement, we could help regions chart a path to sustainable growth and prosperity.

Our Approach

We work closely with regional stakeholders – from economic development organizations and tourism boards to local businesses and community groups – to develop tailored solutions that address their unique challenges and opportunities.

1. Assessment & Strategy

We start by understanding your region's assets, challenges, and goals, then develop a comprehensive strategy for growth.

2. Technology Solutions

We implement innovative tools that connect outdoor enthusiasts with local businesses and gather valuable data to inform decision-making.

3. Partnership Building

We foster relationships between key stakeholders, creating an ecosystem of collaboration and mutual benefit.

4. Sustainable Growth

We establish metrics for success and implement systems that drive long-term economic development while preserving natural resources.

Our Impact

Our partnerships have helped communities:

  • Drive foot traffic to targeted retail locations with +50% of users
  • Generate valuable first-party data for informed decision-making
  • Create new partnerships between outdoor recreation providers and local merchants
  • Distribute tourism impact beyond traditional hotspots and peak season

We're proud of the results we've achieved, but we're just getting started. Our vision is to build a network of thriving regions that leverage their unique outdoor assets to create sustainable economic opportunities for all community members.
